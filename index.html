Hi! I'm Minho :)

My name is Otacílio B. L. Neto, but people call me "Minho".

I am a PhD candidate within the Process Control and Automation Group at Aalto University (Finland). I hold a B.Eng. in Computer Engineering and a M.Sc. in Teleinformatics Engineering from the Federal University of Ceará (Brazil). My research interests lie on the fields of control theory, dynamic game theory and statistical machine learning, with applications to the optimal control and estimation of large-scale (bio)chemical systems. I also have an interest in algorithmic problem solving and graph theory.

In this website, you can find information on my research projects and papers. I am also trying to compile my studies/software into lecture notes. Check also these random stuff!
