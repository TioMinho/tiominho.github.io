Otacílio "Minho" Neto

My name is Otacílio Bezerra Leite Neto, but people call me "Minho".

I am currently a Master's student at the Federal University of Ceará (UFC), in Brazil, within the Postgraduate Programme in Teleinformatics Engineering. My research interests focus on the fields of Optimal Control Theory and Statistical Machine Learning, with applications on the optimal control, identification, and estimation of large-scale systems. Marginally, I also have an interest in algorithmic problem solving and graph theory. I hold a bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering (2019), also from UFC.

One of my biggest interests (besides science) is teaching. I have engaged with several teaching activities and have being a teaching assistant for some courses while still an undergraduate. To help others, I usually write didactic notes and code for the subjects of my studies. These notes are organized in my Notes side-page.
