Ph.D. Candidate | Process Control and Automation
School of Chemical Engineering
Aalto University


I am a scholar of applied mathematics.
My research focus on the intersection of optimal control and dynamic game theory: The goal is to discover equilibrium-seeking algorithms which allow (non-cooperative) agents to learn optimal policies for controlling (decentralised/large-scale/stochastic) networked systems. Applications include (bio)chemical networks, smart city infrastructure, and supply-chains.

When no one is watching, I also spend time studying signal processing, algorithms, statistics, and many other stuff involving maths and computers.
